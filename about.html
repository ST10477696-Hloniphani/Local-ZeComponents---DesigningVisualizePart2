About Local ZeComponents

Local ZeComponents is a community-focused platform where individuals and small teams create and share professional CVs, project portfolios, and hobby showcases to attract local employers, collaborators and clients.

Our Mission

To empower local talent by providing an easy-to-use platform to present professional achievements, practical skills, and personal projects in a discoverable way.

Vision & Values

  • Transparency: Users control what they publish.
  • Accessibility: Profiles must be readable and usable on any device.
  • Community: Promote collaboration and local hiring opportunities.
